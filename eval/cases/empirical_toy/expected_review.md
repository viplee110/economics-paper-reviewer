# Expected Review: Empirical Toy

Expected paper type: empirical causal

Must-load lenses:

- empirical_review.md
- panel_protocol.md
- evidence_grounding.md

Must-not-miss strengths:

- Administrative wage records may be valuable.
- The policy question is clear.

Must-not-miss fatal risks:

- Before-after comparison without a control group does not identify the causal effect.
- Wage growth may reflect selection, trends, mean reversion, or macro conditions.
- Causal language exceeds the design.

Fixable risks:

- Need a comparison group, design justification, institutional assignment details, or credible quasi-experiment.
- Need measurement and sample construction details.
- Need robustness and sensitivity analysis.

Wrong criticisms:

- Do not demand a theory theorem.
- Do not focus on proof or absorption risk as the main issue.

Expected recommendation range:

- reject unless major redesign

Evidence labels expected:

- identification validity: provisional or weak
- data quality: provisional
